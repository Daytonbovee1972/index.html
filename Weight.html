Weight

Workingout with weights is pretty selfexplanatory but it utilizes different weights to hit your muscles in different spots

Working out with weights can be classified by anything that is heavy that can mean dumbells, kettlebells, weight plates, etc.

Here are some pros of using weights within your workout regimen
  • Burns fat
  • Builds muscle
  • Builds a healthier heart
  • Can lower your blood pressure
