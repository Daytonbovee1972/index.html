Cardio

Cardio is the act of using your cardiovascular system during a workout

examples of Cardio are things like running, biking and the elliptical

Here are some pros of using Cardio within your workout regimen
  • Burns fat
  • Can increase your brain activity
  • Can increase your sleep
  • Can lower your blood pressure
